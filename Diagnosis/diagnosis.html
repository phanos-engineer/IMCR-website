Diagnosis

The multidisciplinary team of specialists at IMCRehab, treat advanced non-surgical pain management. In the safety and comfort of a state-of-the-art outpatient setting, we effectively diagnose and treat a wide variety of painful medical conditions.

Some of the condition we treat at Integrated Medical Care & Rehabilitation include but not limited to are listed below:

  • Cancer pain, neuropathic pain
  • Spinal disorders-disc & joint
  • Joint arthritis-shoulder & knee
  • Tendinitis, bursitis
  • Injury-brain, spinal cord
  • Work injury
  • Sport injury
  • Stroke, multiple sclerosis, ALS
  • Acute & chronic muscle disorders
  • CRPS & phantom pain
  • Sacroiliac & hip joint disorder
  • Cervical and lumbar radiculopathy
  • Spasticity & dystonia
  • Headache-migraine, cluster
  • Post surgical, post fusion, discectomy
  • Myopathy, fibromyalgia, myofacial pain
  • Gait & balance disorder
  • Peripheral & entrapment neropathy
  • Head trauma & brain injury
  • Gullian bare, post polio, CIN
  • Additional procedures done in the office:
  • Interventional pain medicine
