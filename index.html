Welcome to Integrated Medical Care & Rehabilitation(IMCR). We are dedicated professionals offering comprehensive musculoskeletal, neuromuscular conditions, diagnostic procedures and rehabilitation, located in Alexandria and Stafford, Virginia. We treat many patients from across the United States and internationally. We serve patients of all ages, specializing in arthritis, sports-related injuries, problems and injuries of the knee, hip, shoulder, foot, wrist, elbow, neck and back pain to just name a few.

We are focused on restoring your independence so you can live more of the life you want. Our quality of care enhances your quality of life.

Our Mission

Is to provide comprehensive musculoskeletal & neuromuscular evaluations and treatments with highly individualized care for each patient.

Dr. Abraham Asmamaw, is a board certified physician in physical medicine and pain medicine who has 15 years experience treating musculoskeletal and neuromuscular disorders.  Dr. Asmamaw is highly qualified and well regarded in his field.

Our Staff including our Physical Therapist and other medical professionals are highly skilled and dedicated to quality patient care.
